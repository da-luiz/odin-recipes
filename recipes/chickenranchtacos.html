Chicken ranch Tacos

Ranch chicken tacos are a tasty variation of tacos featuring chicken seasoned with ranch flavors. Here's a simple recipe to help you create your own ranch chicken tacos:


Ingredients:
For the ranch chiken:
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into strips or chunks
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning mix (or homemade ranch seasoning)
  • Olive oil for cooking

For assembling the tacos:
  • Flour or corn tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions or green onions
  • Grated cheddar cheese or your favorite cheese
  • Ranch dressing
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, coat the chicken pieces with the ranch seasoning mix. Make sure the chicken is well coated.
  2. Heat a skillet or pan over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken to the skillet and cook until the chicken is cooked through and nicely browned, about 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. To assemble the tacos, place a few pieces of ranch-flavored chicken onto each tortilla.
  6. Top the chicken with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced onions, and grated cheese.
  7. Drizzle ranch dressing over the toppings for extra flavor.
  8. Sprinkle chopped cilantro on top and squeeze a bit of lime juice for a fresh kick.
  9. Serve the ranch chicken tacos with lime wedges on the side.

Feel free to customize your tacos with additional ingredients like avocado slices, jalapeños, or black beans, based on your preferences.
These ranch chicken tacos offer a wonderful combination of flavors and textures, and they're sure to be a hit at any meal!
